Northern Cape

Type of Crop Nutritional Information
Wheat Primary source of carbohydrates and dietary fiber. Contains B vitamins (especially folate), iron, and magnesium.
Olives Source of healthy fats (monounsaturated fats), vitamin E, and antioxidants.
Table Grapes Rich in vitamins (C, K), dietary fiber, and antioxidants.
Livestock (Beef, Sheep) Source of high-quality protein, vitamins (B vitamins), and minerals (iron, zinc).
Carrots Rich in vitamin A (beta-carotene), fiber, and antioxidants.
Watermelons Source of vitamins (A, C), dietary fiber, and hydration (water content).
Pecan Nuts High in monounsaturated fats, protein, dietary fiber, and essential minerals like magnesium and manganese.
Onions Source of vitamins (C), dietary fiber, and antioxidants.
