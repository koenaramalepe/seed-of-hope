Type of Crop Nutritional Information
Maize Rich in carbohydrates, fiber, and essential minerals like magnesium and phosphorus.

Dry Beans Good source of protein, dietary fiber, vitamins (B vitamins), and minerals (iron, potassium).
Soybeans High in protein, essential amino acids, healthy fats (omega-3 and omega-6), vitamins (B vitamins), and minerals (iron, calcium).
Potatoes Good source of carbohydrates, dietary fiber, vitamin C, potassium, and B vitamins (especially B6).
Cherries Rich in antioxidants, vitamin C, fiber, and potassium.
