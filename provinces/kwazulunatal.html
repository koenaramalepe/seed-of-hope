KwaZulu-Natal

Type of Crop Nutritional Information
Sugar Cane Source of carbohydrates and used for sugar production.
Soybeans High in protein, essential amino acids, healthy fats (omega-3 and omega-6), vitamins (B vitamins), and minerals (iron, calcium).
Maize Rich in carbohydrates, fiber, and essential minerals like magnesium and phosphorus.
Indigenous Medicinal Plants Used traditionally for medicinal purposes, rich in phytochemicals and antioxidants.
Avocados Rich in healthy fats (monounsaturated fats), fiber, potassium, vitamin K, and folate.
Potatoes Good source of carbohydrates, dietary fiber, vitamin C, potassium, and B vitamins (especially B6).
Macadamia Nuts High in monounsaturated fats, protein, dietary fiber, and essential minerals like magnesium and manganese.
Subtropical Fruits (Mangoes, Bananas) Rich in vitamins (C), dietary fiber, and minerals (potassium).
